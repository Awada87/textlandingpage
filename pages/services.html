Here are our services
The Tenald Group could offer a variety of services aimed at addressing social issues and supporting communities. Here are some potential services:

    - Workshops on life skills, career development, and
    -vocational training.
  2. Healthcare Services
    - Free or low-cost health clinics.
    - Mental health support and counseling services.
  3. Food Security Initiatives
    - Food banks and meal distribution programs.
    - Nutrition education workshops.

    - Support for homeless individuals and families.
    - Affordable housing programs and resources.
    - Emergency shelter service

    These services can be tailored to meet the specific needs of the communities served by the Tenald Group, ensuring that their impact is both relevant and effective.
